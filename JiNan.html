Introduction

Jinan is the capital city of Shandong Province. The resident population is 9.2024 million and the area is 10244.45 square kilometers.


Jinan is located in East China, a warm temperate continental monsoon climate zone with four distinct seasons.

Jinan is an important intersection point of the Army Base of the Northern Theater of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, the Bohai Rim Economic Zone and the Beijing-Shanghai economic axis.
